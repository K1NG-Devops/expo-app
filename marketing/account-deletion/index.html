Request account deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your EduDash Pro account and associated data.

How to request (in‑app)

  • Open the app and sign in
  • Go to Settings → Security & Privacy
  • Tap “Request account deletion”
  • Confirm your request

Alternatively, you can open Account → “Request account deletion”.

What will be deleted

We delete your account and personal data stored in our systems. Some records may be retained where we have a legal obligation (e.g., billing/invoices) or for fraud prevention and security auditing. Such records are kept only as long as legally required and are separated from your account identity as far as possible.

Timeline & retention

Your request is scheduled within 7 days and processed thereafter. Soft‑deleted records may be retained up to 30 days for recovery/security purposes before permanent removal, unless we are required to retain specific records longer for legal compliance.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app, email our support team from your account email with the subject “Account deletion request”.
