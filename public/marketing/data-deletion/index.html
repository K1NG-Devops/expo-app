Request data deletion (keep account)

Use this process to request deletion of selected data categories while keeping your EduDash Pro account active.

How to request (in‑app)

  • Open the app and sign in
  • Go to Settings → Security & Privacy
  • Tap “Request data deletion”
  • Select the categories to delete and submit

What you can request to delete

  • Uploaded files & media (e.g., profile images or other uploads)
  • Messages & communications
  • Activity history & analytics
  • AI-generated content (prompts and outputs)
  • Non‑essential personal data (optional profile fields/preferences)

Some records may need to be retained if we have a legal obligation (e.g., billing/invoices) or for security/fraud prevention. Such retention is minimized and time‑limited.

Timeline & processing

We typically start processing within 7 days. Soft‑deleted records may be retained up to 30 days for recovery/security purposes before permanent removal, unless a longer legal retention period is required.

Can’t access the app?

Email us from your account email with the subject “Partial data deletion request” and list the categories to delete.
